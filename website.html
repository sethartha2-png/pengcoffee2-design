Behind The Brew

Born Out of a Passion for Rich Tradition

Founded in 2018, Aroma & Ember began with a simple belief: coffee isn't just a caffeine delivery mechanism; it is a shared sensory language. We journey across three continents to partner with independent, multi-generational farmers who practice strict micro-lot cultivation.

Back home, our master roasters meticulously map temperature profiles to accentuate unique notes—from bright citrus Ethiopian profiles to dark, chocolaty Colombian roasts.
